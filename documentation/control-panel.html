Control Panel

The Facebook Connect control panel area allows you adjust preferences and run diagnostics.

  • Preferences
  • Diagnostics
  • Demo Templates

Preferences

This is where you can control which member accounts are eligible for FB login, and which member group people are assigned to during passive registration and active registration. If a person belongs to one of the eligible member groups AND they are logged into EE AND they submit the FB login button, their FB ID will be synced with your EE account and they will be able to use it to login in the future. As a security provision, Super Admin accounts can never be synced with Facebook Accounts.

You can also enable Passive Registrations, as well as the option to have EE logged in users be given an additional "confirm" step when they click the Facebook Login button.

Diagnostics

This page allows you to view and run some simple diagnostics on your sites' connection to Facebook.

Demo Templates

If you wish to load the demo templates for Facebook Connect, go to this page. Then just create a prefix name for the sample data such as test_ or fbc_, and click the Install Demo Templates button.
